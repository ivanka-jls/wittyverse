About this place.

Manifesto
ENTERED CYCLE 244
REV. 2 · PUBLIC RECORD
Contents:
One Earth-facing storefront. One alien shopkeeper (Winston). One catalog of items currently in stock and cleared for export.
Purpose:
To sell items from the other side to Earth, on Tuesdays, with provenance, properly inspected, and at a fair price.
Directions:
Browse the catalog. Add items to bag. Check out. Items dispatch from Charlotte on the next Tuesday after order. Returns: write the fulfillment desk and we'll sort it. The contact form in the footer reaches Winston directly.

I'm Winston, and the Wittyverse is my Earth-facing storefront.

A few years back, I came across a portal connecting my side to yours. I went through, took a look at how Earth retail worked, met the people I'd be partnering with on the fulfillment side, and came home. Since then I've been running the Wittyverse: my manufacturing partners on this side produce the catalog, the items cross at the dock on Tuesdays, the Earth-side desk handles your end of things.

I noticed early on that Earth finds the catalog funny. I cannot fully account for this. The Child Chucker is, where I come from, the standard household disciplinary tool — useful, well-made, properly priced. But your market calls these items "gag gifts," prices them as such, and reviews are excellent. I am not in the business of arguing with a paying customer. I sell what sells.

Tuesdays are dispatch days. Most of my week is paperwork, inventory, and correspondence. If you have a question, I'm the right person to ask. The contact form in the footer reaches me directly.

— Winston, Dr. W. Yeti PhD
