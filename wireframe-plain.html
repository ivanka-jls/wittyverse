Design notes — Proof of Import §6
IAMoved before FAQ per IA audit. For a novelty-premise brand, the legitimacy objection ("is this real?") supersedes the process objection ("how does shipping work?"). Visitors must buy the premise before caring about logistics.
IALayout: preamble (Winston's R2 framing) on the left, leaning into the wider column. Guestbook panel on the right, narrow + dark — visually weighted like a sidebar artifact, not the section's main body. The eye reads Winston's argument first, then drops into the proof beside it.
VISUALGuestbook is the page's first piece of explicit visual identity — dark bg, mono type, amber title, green handles. Pattern: leaked terminal feed, not polished review section. The format is the proof — it can't be Earth-side marketing chrome and still feel "leaked from the aggregator." Resist softening it.
COPYOnly 3 entries, only Wittyverse-locals reviewing the 3 imported products (Headlight Fluid, Reality Stabilizer, Dehydrated Water). Tone = banal consumer feedback (winter formula, ratio mislabeling, husband-keeps-two complaint). The joke is the proximity: Earth-side they're gag gifts; over there they're regular household goods with regular gripes. Dropped: Earth-side reactions, Anonymous meta-entries, and reviews of not-imported products (Child Chucker / Eye Bleach) — those belong elsewhere if they appear at all.
VISUALEntry codes (c247.3, c245.8) read as Wittyverse-side timestamps — keep them descending (newer first). Header still says "1,247 ENTRIES" because the panel surfaces 3 of many, implying Winston curated. Footer reads LAST SYNC · c247.4 · WTC 03:41 — same numeric system as the entry codes, plus a Wittyverse Time Coordinated stamp. Makes the panel feel like a real feed pulled minutes ago, not a static testimonial wall.
VISUALDr. W. Yeti's signed stamp sits under the preamble on the left — same circular mark as the masthead but smaller (84px, -6° rotation). It's the visual receipt that ties Winston's R2 framing to the panel's R1 evidence. Position pairs visually with the panel's top-right meta line — left = signature, right = source.
MOTIONGreen live-dot beside the LIVE DISPATCH title pulses (opacity 1↔0.45 + radial glow 0→5px) every 1.6s. Reads as "feed is open." Entries fade in on scroll, top-down, stagger 80ms. Hover entry: slight amber bg lift (rgba(212,160,23,0.06)). Optional click → expands inline with full text + sector code.
MOTIONMini stamp on the left should not animate by default — it's a static seal. Hover: subtle 250ms rotation from -6° to 0° (rights itself, like someone aligning it), back on mouse-leave. Keeps the personality without competing with the live-dot.
COPYThe "lol" cert and the big Amazon-style review cards are both removed. The guestbook panel is now the entire proof artifact. Preamble (Winston's R2 showman voice) carries the framing. Two formats fighting over the same job is gone.
