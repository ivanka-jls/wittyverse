Wittyverse.com — plain copy wireframe (3 options per slot)

Sections per design plan (docs/plans/2026-05-15-wittyverse-landing-v1-design.md). No styling beyond readability. For copy/voice judgment only.

R2 = Dr. Yeti speaking (loud first-person, Popeil × Mays × Barnum × Bronner). R1 = the operation documenting itself (deadpan plural).

Copy direction (calibrated against memory): sell goods, scarcity, guarantee — not the founder myth. Winston is opportunistic, not heroic. Pitchman energy. The bio drips through while the pitch hammers the offer.

"Cycle" purge: removed from all v1 chrome — competes with Tuesday instead of reinforcing it. Tuesday is the ritual. Manifest is the inventory unit. (Cycle stays available as later depth-stack flavor; out of v1 scope.)

Each slot shows three directional variants — A / B / C — built to be remixed. Pick one per slot or steal lines across.

Q&A 1 — speed
AQ: "How fast can you get one of these into a customer's hands? Because I personally hate waiting."
BQ: "Listen — when does my brother-in-law get this in his hands? I will not have him kept waiting."
CQ: "Speed."
A: Orders received by Monday 11:59pm ET ship the following Tuesday from North Carolina. Domestic transit: 3–6 business days. Tracking provided. Tuesdays are the only dispatch window.
Q&A 2 — returns
AQ: "Returns? Yes. Refunds? Also yes. I built this business on guarantees."
BQ: "Look — if the gift doesn't land, I refund it. End of policy."
CQ: "What is your refund policy."
A: Unopened items: 30 days, full refund. Opened items: not returnable (gift-goods classification). Defective items: replaced or refunded at operator discretion.
Q&A 3 — what arrives in the box
AQ: "What's in the box? I want them to feel like a king when they open it."
BQ: "I personally pack every shipment. Tell them what they're getting."
CQ: "Box contents."
A: One unit. Original packaging. Operator-signed dispatch slip. Tracking and dispatch date printed on outer label. No promotional inserts.
Q&A 4 — is this a real store
AQ: "I get asked this constantly. Yes. This is a real store. Real units. Real shipping. I'm a real person."
BQ: "Real store. Real units. Real shipping. I'm pseudonymous, not imaginary."
CQ: "Is this a real store."
A: Wittyverse is a registered LLC in North Carolina. Payments processed by Shopify. Tracking issued by USPS or UPS. Operator pseudonymous (legal name on file with relevant authorities).
Q&A 5 — international shipping
AQ: "International? Currently no. I am not authorized to clear those customs. Yet."
BQ: "We don't ship outside the United States at present. Working on it. — W."
CQ: "Do you ship internationally."
A: Domestic US only at this time. International dispatch pending the relevant paperwork.
Q&A 6 — who is Dr. Yeti (optional)
AQ: "Who am I? The sole authorized importer of Wittyverse goods. Earth side. That is all I'm authorized to say."
BQ: "Look — Dr. W. Yeti PhD, sole importer. The rest is none of anyone's business."

A: Dr. W. Yeti PhD, sole authorized importer. Credentials on file. Further biographical detail withheld at operator discretion.
