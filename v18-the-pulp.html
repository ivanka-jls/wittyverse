A Letter

ENTERED CYCLE 244 · REV. 4
PUBLIC RECORD · MANIFEST 247.04
Wittyverse Press · Editorial Department By Winston, Dr. W. Yeti PhD · Cycle 247

A few years back, I came across a portal connecting my side to yours. I went through, took a look at how Earth retail worked, met the people I'd be partnering with on the fulfillment side, and came home. Since then I have been running the Wittyverse: my manufacturing partners on this side produce the catalog, the items cross at the dock on Tuesdays, and the Earth-side desk handles your end of things.

I noticed early on that Earth finds the catalog funny. I cannot fully account for this. The Child Chucker is, where I come from, the standard household disciplinary tool — useful, well-made, properly priced. Your market calls these items "gag gifts," prices them as such, and the reviews are excellent. I am not in the business of arguing with a paying customer. I sell what sells.

Tuesdays are dispatch days. Most of my week is paperwork, inventory, and correspondence. If you have a question, I am the right person to ask. The contact form in the colophon reaches me directly, not a department.

This issue covers Cycle 247: the annual catalog, the standing advisories, the new arrivals, and a quiet acknowledgment that the Patent Office has, once again, declined to amend its filing on the Dehydrated Water canister. Their stated rationale remains "lol." I have stopped asking.

Order accordingly.

Yours, in good standing with the dock,
Winston, Dr. W. Yeti PhD
